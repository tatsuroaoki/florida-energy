Why Should We Care?

Fossil fuels are not reusable. It is not only damaging to the environment but it will overtime become too expensive due to its limited resources.

Renewable energy will enable us to protect our environment while keeping the cost low because renewable energy can be constantly replenished. As our energy consumption increases each year, we must find a way to keep up with the demand through renewable energy for our future.
